Recipes

Tacos

Ingredients

  • 28g of Fiesta Cheese
  • 8oz of Extra lean ground beef
  • 85g of shredded lettuce
  • 145g of cilantro lime rice

Steps

  1. Cook and drain ground beef
  2. Add taco seasoning packet
  3. Let simmer
  4. Assemble with all ingredients

Macros

  • Calories: 843 (with rice)/ 543 (without)
  • Protein: 73g
  • Carbs: 97g
  • Fat: 30g

"Real Good" Chicken

Ingredients

  • 8oz of Real Good Chicken
  • 200g of Uncle Bens Spanich Rice
  • 85g of Steamed Brocoli

Steps

  1. Prep oven to 400 F
  2. Cook chicken for 20 minutes
  3. Steam brocoli for 6 minutes
  4. Steam rice for 2 minutes
  5. Portion into meal prep

Macros

  • Calories: 549
  • Protein: 56g
  • Carbs: 64g
  • Fat: 7g

Bagel with eggs

Ingredients

  • 3 Large eggs
  • 1 Great Value Bagel
  • 45g of egg whites
  • 2tbsp of Cream Cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat skillet on medium heat
  2. Crack 3 eggs + pour egg whites into skillet
  3. Place bagel in toaster
  4. Season eggs with S+P then cover eggs
  5. Spread Cream Cheese on bagel

Macros

  • Calories: 748
  • Protein: 37g
  • Carbs: 47g
  • Fat: 26g
