About Me

My name is Tucker Vandament. I have recently started learning how to code in HTML, CSS and JavaScript for the past couple months now. Hopefully, this leads to me changing careers. For a little work background, almost all of my jobs have been centered around customer support. Whether that is from being a server/bartender, or working in tech support call centers with Dell and AT&T, to recently being a manager in retail. While the jobs have been good, I never truly felt challenged or fulfilled. While in college I was working towards a bachelors in Computer Science. It wasn't until recently that I started learning the beginning stages of front-end development, that I have throughouly enjoyed it.



The purpose of this website is to showcase to myself the things I have learned. Whether that is through new features or techniques. On the website I hope to build a photo and video album showcasing me throughout the years, whether that is photos of just me or random things. I will include a recipe page, a place I will store recipes and add a list that will get said recipes allowing an easy way to remake them in the future. Overall, I hope this will become a good foundation of my journey and will really show myself the things I have learned along the way.
